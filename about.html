Our Story

Founded in 1995, Dr. Ambedkar Education Trust is dedicated to fostering education, social equity, and sustainable development. Inspired by the teachings of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, we strive to build an inclusive society where every individual has the opportunity to realize their full potential.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower marginalized groups through education, advocacy, and sustainable development initiatives. We are committed to breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for all, ensuring a progressive and just society.

Our Vision

We envision a world where social equity prevails, and every individual, regardless of background, can thrive. Through our efforts, we aim to create lasting, positive change that resonates across generations.

Our Core Values

  • Equality: We believe in equal opportunities for all, and work to eliminate social and economic disparities.
  • Education: Education is the cornerstone of empowerment. We provide access to quality education and vocational training.
  • Sustainability: Our programs are designed with long-term sustainability in mind, benefiting communities now and in the future.
  • Community Engagement: We work closely with local communities, ensuring our initiatives are relevant and impactful.
